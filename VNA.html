Vector Network Analyser

Skills: Analogue Design, LTSpice XVII, MATLAB;

This project was the final coursework for my Instrumentation Module.

The task was to design and simulate a Network Analyser capable of calculating the Y and Z parameters of a 2 port device. This project involved heavy use of LTSpice and MATLAB. LTSpice was used to design and simulate the system and create/collect the data for the circuits. The data was then imported into MATLAB where the functions of the Vector Network Analyser were then simulated in a MATLAB script to calculate the Y and Z parameters.

Below are lower level designs outlining the specific building blocks used for the overall system.

Differential Amp: Amplifies the voltage differences across the current sense resistors

2nd Order Low Pass Filter

Mixer: basis of In-Phase and Quadrature modulation blocks
